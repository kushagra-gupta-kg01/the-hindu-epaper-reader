A man who had allegedly been treating patients without a medical degree for six years in Gurugram was arrested during a raid by officials late on Tuesday night, the police said on Wednesday. Based on a complaint by a medical officer, a case was registered against Sukumar Biswas at the Bajghera police station under relevant sections of the Medical Council Act and under the BNS sections pertaining to cheating and fraud.
