# Utilise budget funds or brace for cuts next year, CM tells officials

**Author:** The Hindu Bureau | **Location:** New Delhi

---

![Article Image](../../images/GKLG1M8CF1GO1G1NR2P1jpg.jpg)

![Article Image](../../images/GKLG1M8CF1GO1G1NR2P1jpg.jpg)

Chief Minister Rekha Gupta on Wednesday held a review meeting with the heads of all departments and directed them to implement all projects mentioned in the annual budget within the stipulated timelines.

The CM directed the officials to expedite cleaning of drains, pending works in schools, and plantation drives before monsoon, read a statement from her office. Departments that fail to properly utilise funds could face cuts in the next budget, warned Ms. Gupta. She said preventing waterlogging remains a priority and laxity would not be accepted.

Large-scale plantation drives should be undertaken in areas with high dust pollution, said the CM while underscoring the need for better coordination among agencies.

She also instructed the department heads to ensure timely whitewashing of government schools, repair of toilets, and clean drinking water for children. Ms. Gupta underlined the importance of providing students with a better and more dignified environment.

Officials were also directed to accelerate schemes related to solar power and water conservation to make Delhi a model city in energy conservation and water management.
