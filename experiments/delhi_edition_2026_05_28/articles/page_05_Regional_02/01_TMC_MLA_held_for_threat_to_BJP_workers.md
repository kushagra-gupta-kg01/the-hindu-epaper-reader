After days of evading police, Trinamool Congress MLA from Bishnupur Dilip Mondal was on Wednesday arrested by the West Bengal police from Puri in Odisha.

He had allegedly threatened Bharatiya Janata Party workers during the Assembly poll.

There have also been mass resignations in the TMC. Senior party leader Kakoli Ghosh Dastidar, rSushanta Ghosh and Arup Chakraborty quit from their respective posts.
