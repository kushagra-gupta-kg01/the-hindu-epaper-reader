The Rajasthan High Court on Wednesday upheld the conviction and life sentence of self-styled godman Asaram for rape of a teenage girl in his ashram near Jodhpur in 2013. In a partial relief, the court acquitted Asaram of charges related to gang rape and gang penetrative sexual assault on a child under the Indian Penal Code and POCSO Act. The court directed Asaram to surrender in the Jodhpur Central Jail while dismissing his appeal against conviction. The 86-year-old self-proclaimed godman is at present out on bail till July 7 on medical grounds, as he has repeatedly sought bail citing his advanced age.
