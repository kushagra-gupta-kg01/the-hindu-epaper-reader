Share of allotted EV stations under MCD yet to become official

50 in per cent. More than half of the EV charging stations allotted under the MCD are yet to become operational due to delays in obtaining approvals from the Public Works Department, officials said on Wednesday. According to official data, a total of 1,047 EV charging stations have been allotted across the city. But, only over 460 stations have so far been made operational. PTI
