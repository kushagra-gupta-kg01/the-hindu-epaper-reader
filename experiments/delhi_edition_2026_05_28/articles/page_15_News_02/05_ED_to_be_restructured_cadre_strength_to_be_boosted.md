# ED to be restructured, cadre strength to be boosted

**Author:** The Hindu Bureau | **Location:** NEW DELHI

---

The Union Finance Ministry sanctioned a sweeping cadre restructuring of the Enforcement Directorate on Wednesday, expanding its current strength by almost 60% from 2,029 to 3,256 posts.

The agencyâs executive cadre will nearly double from 1,229 to 2,288 posts.

The most dramatic changes have been approved in the executive cadre, which forms the operational backbone of the ED. While special directors remain unchanged at seven, additional directors have increased from 10 to 24; joint directors from 28 to 49; and deputy directors from 148 to 267.

The sanctioned strength of assistant directors has been revised from 255 to 531; enforcement officers from 355 to 606; and assistant enforcement officers from 425 to 803, nearly doubling the sanctioned posts. These three posts are key to investigations.
