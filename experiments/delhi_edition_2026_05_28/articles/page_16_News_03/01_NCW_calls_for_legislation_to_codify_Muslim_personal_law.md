# NCW calls for legislation to codify Muslim personal law

---

The National Commission for Women (NCW) has submitted a report on the rights of Muslim women in India to key central Ministries. The report recommends a comprehensive legislation to codify Muslim personal law related to marriage, divorce, maintenance, custody, and inheritance, strengthening provisions for maintenance during and after marriage, and mandatory registration of marriages. It also sought the establishment of gender-sensitive dispute resolution mechanisms with adequate representation for women and accountability to civil courts, expansion of legal aid systems, helplines, and targeted awareness campaigns.
