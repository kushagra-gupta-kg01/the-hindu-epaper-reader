# Centre seeks transfer of Transgender Act cases to SC

---

The Centre urged the Supreme Court on Wednesday to transfer petitions challenging the constitutionality of the Transgender Persons (Protection of Rights) Amendment Act, 2026 from about four different High Courts to the apex court. The Supreme Court is already seized of several petitions challenging the 2026 Act, but it is is only partially working during June. The petitions in the SC have criticised the controversial 2026 law of removing the right to self-identity.
