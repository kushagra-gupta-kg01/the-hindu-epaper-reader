# LIC unveils two new joint life insurance policies

**Author:** The Hindu Bureau | **Location:** Hyderabad

---

The Life Insurance Corporation of India (LIC) has introduced two new plans New Jeevan Sathi-Single Premium and New Jeevan Sathi-Limited Premium.

Both are non-par, non-linked, individual, savings plans. These are joint life plans which offer a combination of savings and protection, LIC said after CEO and MD R. Doraiswamy launched the new plans.
