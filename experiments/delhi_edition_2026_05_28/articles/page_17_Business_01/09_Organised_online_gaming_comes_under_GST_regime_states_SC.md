# Organised online gaming comes under GST regime, states SC

**Location:** New Delhi

The Supreme Court on Wednesday confirmed the constitutional validity of bringing organised online gaming activities with money stakes under the Goods and Services Tax (GST) regime.

A Bench of Justices J.B. Pardiwala and R. Mahadevan, in a judgment, declined challenges raised against the levy of GST on actionable claims arising from organised online gaming activities and fantasy sports under the betting and gambling head.

The court reasoned that even if online gaming involves skill, there was substantial money involved and an uncertainty at stake on the outcome.

The court said the States had a duty to maintain public tranquillity and public health, considering the growing problems of addiction and deaths due to financial losses suffered online. The court said the Tamil Nadu law was based on empirical data collected through a panel chaired by a former Madras High Court judge.
