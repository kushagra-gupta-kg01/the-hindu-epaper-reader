# Karn leads the way for Khushal Cricket in chase

---

Khushal Cricket CC rode on an unbeaten 87 from Karn Kashyap to beat Bright CC by six wickets in the 49th Lala Raghubir Singh Hot Weather cricket tournament at Modern School in New Delhi on Wednesday.

The scores: Bright 196 in 34.2 overs (Vansh Mehra 40, Rushal 34, Pioneer Joshi 3/22) lost to Khushal Cricket 199/4 in 22.3 overs (Karn Kashyap 87 n.o., Jaskaran Singh 44).
